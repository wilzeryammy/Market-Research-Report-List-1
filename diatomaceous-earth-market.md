<p><p>The diatomaceous earth market comprises two primary types: diatomite particles and diatomite powder. Diatomite particles refer to larger, unprocessed fragments used in various industrial applications, providing filtration and absorption properties. In contrast, diatomite powder is finely ground, offering greater versatility and surface area, making it ideal for applications like cosmetics, agriculture, and food processing. Both forms leverage the unique silica structure of diatomaceous earth, catering to diverse sectors, including construction, environmental solutions, and personal care products.</p></p>
