<p><p>The epoxidized soybean oil digomer market features two main production methods: ring-opening polymerization and reaction with maleic anhydride. Ring-opening polymerization involves the transformation of epoxidized soybean oil into high-performance polymers, enhancing flexibility and stability. In contrast, the reaction with maleic anhydride creates a cross-linked network, boosting the material's strength and chemical resistance. Both methods cater to different application needs in industries such as coatings, adhesives, and plastics, highlighting the versatility of epoxidized soybean oil digomers.</p></p>
