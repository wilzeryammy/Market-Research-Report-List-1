<p><ul><li>Grid-Tied Solar Home Lighting</li><li>Off-Grid Solar Home Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar home lighting market primarily comprises two types: grid-tied and off-grid solar home lighting systems. Grid-tied solar home lighting systems are connected to the public electricity grid, allowing homeowners to utilize both solar energy and grid power, often leading to reduced electricity costs. In contrast, off-grid solar home lighting systems operate independently from the grid, using standalone solar panels and batteries to provide electricity in remote areas, offering a reliable power source where traditional electricity access is limited.</p></p>
