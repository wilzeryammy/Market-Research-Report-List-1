<p><ul><li>Wet End Equipment</li><li>Dry End Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrugating machinery market is divided into two primary types: wet end equipment and dry end equipment. Wet end equipment involves machines that handle the initial stages of producing corrugated board, including the preparation and layering of paper. In contrast, dry end equipment focuses on the finishing processes, such as cutting, gluing, and stacking the final corrugated product. Both segments play essential roles in manufacturing efficient and high-quality corrugated packaging solutions for various industries.</p></p>
