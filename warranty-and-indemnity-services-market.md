<p><p>The Warranty and Indemnity Services market encompasses two main types: Warranty Services and Indemnity Services. Warranty Services involve guarantees provided by sellers to buyers, ensuring that specific conditions will be met, such as product quality or performance. Indemnity Services, on the other hand, offer protection against future losses or damages arising from breaches or liabilities. These services are crucial in transactions, particularly in mergers and acquisitions, where they mitigate risks and enhance buyer confidence during negotiations and post-deal integration.</p></p>
