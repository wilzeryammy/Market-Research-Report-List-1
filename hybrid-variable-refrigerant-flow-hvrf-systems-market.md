<p><p>Hybrid Variable Refrigerant Flow (HVRF) systems comprise two primary market types: Outdoor Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) systems and Indoor Variable Refrigerant Flow systems. Outdoor VRF systems include the external condensing units that manage refrigerant distribution and optimize energy efficiency. In contrast, Indoor VRF systems encompass various air handling units designed for precise temperature control and comfort within spaces. Together, these components enhance heating and cooling performance while allowing for flexible installation in diverse building environments.</p></p>
