<p><p>The Zirconium Silicate Bead market is primarily divided into two types: Sintered Zirconium Silicate and Fused Zirconium Silicate. Sintered Zirconium Silicate beads are produced through a high-temperature sintering process, resulting in durable beads suitable for various grinding and milling applications. In contrast, Fused Zirconium Silicate beads are manufactured by melting zirconium silicate at high temperatures, offering enhanced density and resilience. Both types are popular in industries such as ceramics, coatings, and abrasives due to their effective performance and superior particle size distribution.</p></p>
