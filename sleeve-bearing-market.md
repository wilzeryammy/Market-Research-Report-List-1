<p><ul><li>Radial Sleeve Bearing</li><li>Axial Sleeve Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sleeve bearing market is divided into radial and axial types, each serving distinct applications. Radial sleeve bearings support loads perpendicular to their shaft, commonly found in electric motors and pumps, offering low friction and high durability. In contrast, axial sleeve bearings manage loads parallel to the shaft, ideal for thrust applications like gearboxes and turbines. Both types emphasize reliability and efficiency, catering to industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, where performance and maintenance are critical.</p></p>
