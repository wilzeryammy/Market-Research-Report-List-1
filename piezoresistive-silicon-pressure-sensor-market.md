<p><strong>The Piezoresistive Silicon Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Pressure Sensors</li><li>Relative (Gauge) Pressure Sensors</li><li>Differential Pressure Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoresistive silicon pressure sensor market includes three main types: absolute, relative (gauge), and differential pressure sensors. Absolute pressure sensors measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, making them ideal for applications requiring precise measurements. Relative pressure sensors gauge atmospheric pressure, useful in applications where fluctuation is a concern. Differential pressure sensors compare two pressure points, often used in flow measurement and filtration processes. Each type serves distinct purposes across various industries, including automotive, healthcare, and industrial automation.</p></p>
