<p><p>The Birth Simulator market is categorized into two main types: Pelvis simulators and Whole Body simulators. Pelvis simulators focus specifically on the pelvic region, allowing users to understand birthing mechanics, practice delivery techniques, and assess fetal positioning. Whole Body simulators, on the other hand, provide a comprehensive training experience by simulating an entire pregnant woman, enabling practitioners to engage in realistic labor scenarios, manage complications, and enhance their overall obstetric skills. Each type serves distinct educational purposes in maternal care.</p></p>
