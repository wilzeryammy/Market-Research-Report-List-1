DC Type monitors operate on direct current, making them portable and suitable for emergency use. AC Type monitors rely on alternating current, offering consistent performance in hospital settings. The DC & AC Type combines both power sources, providing versatility for varied clinical environments, ensuring reliable monitoring whether in transit or stationary, enhancing patient care and accessibility.</p></p>
