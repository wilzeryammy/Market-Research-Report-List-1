<p><strong>The Electronic Fragrance Diffuser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Diffuser</li><li>Evaporation Diffuser</li><li>Electrothermal Diffuser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic fragrance diffuser market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Ultrasonic Diffusers** use ultrasonic waves to create a fine mist of water and essential oils, dispersing fragrance effectively.</p><p>2. **Evaporation Diffusers** rely on airflow to disperse the fragrance as the oils evaporate, providing a simple, heat-free option.</p><p>3. **Electrothermal Diffusers** utilize heat to evaporate and release fragrances, offering consistent scent diffusion but may alter the oil's properties. Each type caters to different consumer preferences for fragrance delivery and ambiance enhancement.</p></p>
