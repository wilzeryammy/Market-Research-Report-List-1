<p><strong>The Fume Hoods Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ductless Fume Hoods</li><li>Ducted Fume Hoods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fume hood market is primarily divided into two types: ductless and ducted fume hoods. Ducted fume hoods are connected to an external ventilation system, effectively expelling hazardous fumes outside, making them suitable for handling toxic substances. On the other hand, ductless fume hoods utilize built-in filtration systems to purify the air before recirculating it back into the lab environment, offering flexibility and ease of installation. Both types play critical roles in ensuring safety in laboratories and industrial settings.</p></p>
