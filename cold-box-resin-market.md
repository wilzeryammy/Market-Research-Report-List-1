<p><ul><li>Mold Sand Casting</li><li>Core Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cold Box Resin market is primarily divided into two main types: Mold Sand Casting and Core Sand Casting. Mold Sand Casting involves creating molds from a mixture of sand and a cold-setting resin, allowing for complex shapes and smooth surfaces, ideal for producing high-quality metal parts. In contrast, Core Sand Casting utilizes similar resin-coated sand to produce internal cavities in castings, enhancing structural integrity and design flexibility. Both methods benefit from reduced cycle times and improved dimensional accuracy in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
