<p><p>Terahertz communication systems encompass a range of components essential for efficient data transmission. Emitters generate terahertz waves, enabling high-speed communication over short distances. Modulators adjust the signal's amplitude, frequency, or phase, allowing for information encoding and enhancing signal quality. Together, these elements form the backbone of terahertz communication systems, facilitating advancements in wireless networks, imaging, and security applications.</p></p>
