<p><p>The non-metal strapping market primarily comprises PP (Polypropylene) and PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) strapping. PP strapping is lightweight, cost-effective, and commonly used for bundling and palletizing due to its flexibility and resistance to moisture. In contrast, PET strapping offers higher strength and durability, making it suitable for heavy loads and demanding applications. Both types serve various industries, including packaging and logistics, where securing products efficiently is essential for transport and storage, ensuring safety and integrity throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
