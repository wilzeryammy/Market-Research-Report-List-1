<p><ul><li>Low Vacuum</li><li>Medium Vacuum</li><li>High Vacuum</li><li>Ultra-High Vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum blower market is categorized into four types based on pressure levels. Low vacuum systems operate at pressures above atmospheric conditions, suitable for tasks like packaging and material handling. Medium vacuum systems function at reduced pressures, ideal for processes such as drying and distillation. High vacuum systems reach substantially lower pressures, used in applications like semiconductor manufacturing and research. Ultra-high vacuum systems achieve extreme pressure reductions, essential for advanced scientific research and technologies like electron microscopy.</p></p>
