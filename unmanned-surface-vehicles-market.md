<p><p>The unmanned surface vehicles (USVs) market is primarily divided into two types: wave-powered and propeller-driven. Wave-powered USVs harness ocean energy for propulsion, promoting sustainability and reducing operational costs. They are ideal for long-duration missions with minimal human intervention. In contrast, propeller-driven USVs utilize conventional motor-driven systems, offering higher speed and maneuverability, making them suitable for a range of applications, including surveillance, environmental monitoring, and research. Together, these technologies enhance maritime operations and research capabilities.</p></p>
