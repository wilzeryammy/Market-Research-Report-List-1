<p><p>The Mobile Identification Solutions market is divided into two primary types: Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) and Single-Factor Authentication (SFA). MFA enhances security by requiring multiple verification methods, such as a password combined with biometrics or a one-time code, reducing the risk of unauthorized access. In contrast, SFA relies on a single verification method, typically a password, making it simpler but less secure. Both methods cater to varying security needs, balancing convenience and protection for users.</p></p>
