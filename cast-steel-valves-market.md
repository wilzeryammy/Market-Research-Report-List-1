<p><p>The cast steel valves market encompasses several key types, including cast steel gate valves, globe valves, and check valves. Cast steel gate valves are designed for on/off control, providing minimal flow resistance when fully open. Globe valves are used for throttling and flow regulation, offering precise control. Check valves prevent backflow, ensuring unidirectional flow in piping systems. Each type is crucial in various industries, including oil and gas, water treatment, and power generation, due to their durability and performance under high pressure and temperature conditions.</p></p>
