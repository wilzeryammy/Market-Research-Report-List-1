Spectrometers analyze light emission to detect chemical compositions and materials. Thermal cameras capture heat distribution to identify overheating components. Corrosion probes measure material degradation due to corrosive environments. Vibration sensors detect abnormal vibrations, indicating potential mechanical issues. Spectrum analyzers evaluate frequency signals, revealing operational abnormalities. Ultrasonic detectors identify high-frequency sound waves to diagnose leaks or mechanical faults, enhancing predictive maintenance strategies across industries.</p></p>
