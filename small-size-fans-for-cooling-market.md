<p><p>The small size fans for cooling market primarily includes axial and centrifugal fans. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, making them ideal for cooling in compact spaces due to their straightforward design and efficiency. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, draw air in through the intake and expel it at a right angle, offering higher pressure and airflow for applications requiring robust ventilation. Together, these fan types cater to diverse cooling needs in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
