<p><p>The stacked CMOS sensor market is divided into 2-layer and 3-layer configurations. Two-layer stacked sensors combine photodetectors and processing circuits, enhancing image quality while reducing space and power consumption. Three-layer stacked sensors add an additional layer, typically for further processing or additional features like improved low-light performance, enabling advanced capabilities in applications such as smartphones, automotive, and surveillance. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of performance, miniaturization, and efficiency, catering to diverse consumer and industrial needs.</p></p>
