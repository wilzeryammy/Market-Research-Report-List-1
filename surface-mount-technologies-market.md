<p><p>The Surface Mount Technologies (SMT) market encompasses several key areas: </p><p>1. **Placement** involves the accurate positioning of surface mount components onto printed circuit boards.</p><p>2. **Inspection** ensures quality control through automated optical and X-ray systems that detect defects.</p><p>3. **Soldering** refers to the process of joining components with solder, using techniques like reflow and wave soldering.</p><p>4. **Screen Printing** applies solder paste onto PCBs for component placement.</p><p>5. **Cleaning** removes flux residues for optimal performance. </p><p>6. **Repair & Rework** services address defects or modifications, ensuring longevity and functionality of assemblies.</p></p>
