<p><ul><li>Dry-Type Flame Arresters</li><li>Wet-Type Flame Arresters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flame Arresters market is categorized into two main types: Dry-Type and Wet-Type Flame Arresters. Dry-Type Flame Arresters utilize a series of metal mesh or baffle systems to extinguish flames, effectively preventing their propagation in gas lines. In contrast, Wet-Type Flame Arresters incorporate a liquid barrier, which cools and suppresses flames more effectively in certain applications, especially in liquid fuel environments. Both types are crucial in enhancing safety in industrial processes by minimizing the risk of explosions.</p></p>
