<p><ul><li>Nerve Conduit</li><li>Nerve Wrap</li><li>Nerve Graft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The peripheral nerve repair market comprises three main types: nerve conduits, nerve wraps, and nerve grafts. Nerve conduits serve as artificial channels that guide regenerating axons across gaps in injured nerves. Nerve wraps are biocompatible materials that encase damaged nerves, promoting healing and preventing scarring. Nerve grafts involve the transplantation of nerve tissue from a donor site to repair severed nerves. Each type addresses different severities of nerve injuries, facilitating optimal recovery and function restoration.</p></p>
