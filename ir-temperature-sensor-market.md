<p><ul><li>Contact IR Temperature Sensor</li><li>Non-contact IR Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IR temperature sensor market comprises two primary types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact IR temperature sensors require physical contact with the object to measure temperature, providing precise readings but limiting their application in certain environments. In contrast, non-contact IR temperature sensors detect the infrared radiation emitted by an object, allowing for accurate temperature measurement from a distance, making them ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. Both types serve various industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, and automotive.</p></p>
