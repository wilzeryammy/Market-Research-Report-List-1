<p><strong>The Synthetic Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic brake fluid market is segmented into four main types: DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 is glycol-based and suitable for general use, while DOT 4 offers higher boiling points and improved performance, making it ideal for vehicles subjected to heavy braking. DOT 5 is silicone-based, providing excellent moisture resistance, but is not compatible with other types. DOT 5.1, also glycol-based, combines the advantages of DOT 4 and DOT 5, catering to high-performance applications.</p></p>
