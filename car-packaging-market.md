<p><p>The car packaging market mainly consists of two types: vinyl and cast vinyl. Cast vinyl, on the other hand, is a high-quality, thinner material that conforms better to curves and contours, making it ideal for long-lasting applications. Cast vinyl offers superior durability and resistance to fading, making it a preferred choice for professional vehicle wrapping projects.</p></p>
